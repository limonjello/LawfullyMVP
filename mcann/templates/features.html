What is Lawful.ly?

Your source for plain language, annotated standard legal documents

When you have a basic question or legal need, you don't want to read a legal treatise or even a legal blog if you don't absolutely have to. Well, neither do we. By using annotations, we believe that the community can provide quick explanations of key terms and overview of the key issues with any document, statute, case, etc.

An open community for sharing legal information

We hope that you will give as much as you take from Lawful.ly. We believe that it's not only lawyers who have valuable information to share about legal documents and terms. In fact, those who have been involved in the legal transactions before may have even better insights or be able to better explain the "real world" meanings of particular legal terms or issues. While we we intend this to be a community with plenty of lawyers, we don't believe the law belongs exclusively to the lawyers.

Your source for legal information, but not legal advice.

We hate to get all "lawyerly" here, but we do want to be clear that Lawful.ly's goal is to empower you with information about the law, not provide you with tailored legal advice. Your specific issues may still require the services of a qualified attorney. However, by providing you with information about the law, we hope to make your interactions with your attorney more efficient (read: cheaper).
